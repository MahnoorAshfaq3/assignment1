Chocolate Chip Cookies

The best homemade cookies you'll ever taste!

Ingredients (for 12 cookies):

  • 1 cup (120g) all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 cup (115g) unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips

Method (Step by Step):

  1. Preheat oven to 180oC (350oF).
  2. Mix flour, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Cream butter and sugars until smooth.
  4. Add egg and vanilla, mix well.
  5. Combine with dry ingredients, fold in chocolate chips.
  6. Bake 10-12 mins until golden brown.
